About Us

Founded in January 2020, Keynes is growing into one of the most trusted names in the Dry Foods Industry. In just 5 years, we have expanded our reach from local markets to international exporters, delivering high-quality dried products to customers across the globe. From the UK to Canada, Germany, the Middle East, and beyond, our commitment to quality is recognized worldwide.

What We Do

We specialize in drying a wide variety of food products, including tapioca, jackfruit, cocoa, grains, and other vegetables. Our expertise lies in the careful handling, hygienic processing, and efficient drying of raw products. We take great pride in offering organic, pesticide-free products to our customers. Our operations are designed with precision to deliver the best quality in every batch.

Our Capacity

At Keynes, we are equipped with both large and small machines to cater to different drying needs:

  • Our large machine can handle up to 3 tons of raw products at a time, making it ideal for bulk food processing like tapioca, cocoa, grains, and other heavy vegetables.
  • For lighter products like jackfruit, we use a smaller machine that can dry up to 50 kg at a time, ensuring quality and efficiency.

Why Choose Us?

We offer competitive prices without compromising on quality. Our pricing is more affordable compared to other market players, giving you great value for your investment. Additionally, we maintain strict hygiene standards to ensure that all products are handled safely and cleanly throughout the drying process.

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in the use of organic methods. We don't use any synthetic fertilizers or pesticides, ensuring that the products we deliver are as natural as possible. This makes our products a healthier option for consumers and a better choice for the environment.
